# Form radiofield group

Renders a form radiofield group.

```htmlbars
<Rose::Form::Radio::Group
  @disabled={{false}}
  @name={{name}}
  @selectedValue="disagree"
  as |radioGroup|
>
  <radioGroup.radio
    @id="agree-radiofield"
    @label="Agree"
    @value="agree"
  />
  <radioGroup.radio
    @id="disagree-radiofield"
    @label="disagree"
    @value="disagree"
  />
  <radioGroup.radio
    @id="maybe-radiofield"
    @label="maybe"
    @value="maybe"
  />
</Rose::Form::Radio::Group>
```
